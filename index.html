How It Works?

Get started in minutes - no complex setup no steep learningcurve.

Step 1

Connect your design project

Import screen from Figma, Sketch or Adobe XD in seconds. DesignIQ automatically organize your work and prepares it for tracking, no setup required.

Step 2

Track engagement & progress

View real-time analytics on user interactions, navigation flow, and design performance. Monitor heatmaps, session recordings, and usability scores.

Step 3

Improve & collaborate

Use insights to refine designs and share progress with your team. Share reports with stakeholders, run A/B tests, and iterate with confidence.
